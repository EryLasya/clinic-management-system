Clinic Information

clinic is a healthcare facility for outpatient care, offering treatment for common illnesses, check-ups, and specialized services without overnight stays, unlike larger hospitals. They range from small doctor's offices to large centers for areas like physical therapy, dental, or mental health, focusing on primary or specific needs in local communities. Clinics provide convenient access to healthcare, handling routine needs and some urgent, but non-life-threatening, conditions.
